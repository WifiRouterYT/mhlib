ABOUT MH LIBRARY

At the library students, staff, and families are welcome to utilize our library services, from checking out novels and textbooks to researching, writing, printing, and getting homework help. We even have vending machines for pens & pencils.


Using their 5-digit student ID #, students can check out up to two books for up to three weeks.

Students can also renew their book(s) one time for an additional 3 weeks when needed.


We are open Monday through Friday before school, during class periods & READ period with a hall pass, at lunch, and after school.


LUNCHES IN THE LIBRARY: After students eat lunch in the designated areas outside on the blacktop they may visit the library for the remainder of lunch. Lunches in the library allow students to play games, read, create, and socialize with others in a safe, comfortable, and inclusive environment.
